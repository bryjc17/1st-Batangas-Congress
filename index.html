Mission Brief

üõ°Ô∏è **Batangas AI and Cybersecurity Congress 2025** üõ°Ô∏è
Join us for this highly anticipated congress, a premier regional event dedicated to the groundbreaking exploration of the intersection between **Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Cybersecurity**. This crucial platform unites **industry leaders, pioneering researchers, and ambitious students** from across the Philippines and the wider region. Our collective mission is to thoroughly discuss and dissect the latest **innovations, critical challenges, and far-reaching implications** in these rapidly evolving technological fields, driving the future of digital defense in Southern Luzon.

Featured Speakers

Mr. Jerry Sto. Tomas
CEO - FusionNode, Inc.

Industry leader in technology innovation and cybersecurity solutions with extensive experience in enterprise security architecture. His session focuses on leveraging **AI for Predictive Threat Hunting**.

Ms. Elvie Pita
Head of the Innovative Learning Unit - University of Batangas

Expert in educational technology and innovative learning methodologies, pioneering AI integration in education. Ms. Pita will discuss **Securing the Academic Network and Ethical AI in Learning Environments**.

Mr. Anthony Vhic R. Cedo, LPT
Education Technology Operations Manager, RAKSO CT

Anthony Vhic Cedo is a licensed professional teacher and multi-certified EdTech advocate. He has extensive experience training school leaders and faculty on productivity tools and **Artificial Intelligence in Education**. His session focuses on the pedagogical security aspects of AI.

Mr. Carlo Alviar
Systems Engineering Expert, Globe Business

With 15 years of ICT experience in Technical Sales and Solution Consulting, Mr. Alviar holds multiple certifications in Cloud, Wired & Wireless, and Cybersecurity services. He will share expertise on **Enterprise Cybersecurity Solutions and AI-Enabled Network Protection**.

Core Topics

  • **AI-powered Threat Detection** and Automated Response Systems
  • Advanced **Machine Learning Algorithms** in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics
  • **Ethical Implications** and Governance of Autonomous AI Systems
  • Data Privacy Protection and **Secure AI Architecture** Design Protocols
  • Future Trajectories of **Adversarial AI** and Mitigation Strategies

Target Operatives

  • Computer Engineering Students and Researchers
  • **Cybersecurity Professionals** and Analysts
  • AI Researchers and **Machine Learning Engineers**
  • IT Managers, **CIOs/CISOs**, and Technology Enthusiasts
